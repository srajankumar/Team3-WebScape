Recycling Process

Collection

E-waste is collected from various sources, including households, businesses, and electronic manufacturers.

Sorting

Collected e-waste is sorted into different categories based on the type of electronics.

Dismantling

Electronics are dismantled to extract valuable components and separate them from hazardous materials.

Component Recovery

Valuable components like metals, plastics, and circuit boards are recovered for recycling.

Hazardous Material Handling

Hazardous materials, including lead and mercury, are carefully handled and disposed of according to regulations.

Recycling Completion

The materials are processed, recycled, and reused, contributing to a more sustainable and eco-friendly approach.
